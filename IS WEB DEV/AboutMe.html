Keaton Dallimore



Keaton Dallimore is 28 years old. He has held many titles in his 28 years. Those titles include athlete while he played football, basketball and baseball since he was a young child all through highschool. Ref, for his first job was a referee and umpire in his youth for young children. Elder, for when he served his mission for the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter Day Saints. Soldier, when he was in the Utah Army National guard as a human intelligence collector. But his favorite titles are Husband and Father. Keaton has been married to his highchool sweetheart, Sydney, for over 7 years and is the father to a 4 year old Charlotte "Charlie" and a 1 year old Donny. Sydney and Keaton are also expecting their 3rd child this summer. Keaton has recently returned to BYU to finish his bachelors degree in General Studies with an emphasis in Business.
